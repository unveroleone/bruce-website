Why Bruce?

⚡ Open-Source

Bruce is licensed under AGPL, making it freely available for the community.

🖥️ Cross-Platform

Bruce runs seamlessly on M5Stack, LilyGo, and other ESP32-based devices.

📶 Wi-Fi Attacks

Supports Beacon-Spam, Deauth Attacks, Evil Portal, and more.

📡 RFID & NFC

Read, write, and clone RFID/NFC tags with advanced NDEF features.

💻 BadUSB & DuckyScript

Execute automated payloads using BadUSB functionality.

🚀 Active Community

Regular updates and community-driven improvements.
